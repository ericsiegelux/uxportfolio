About Me

My name is Eric Siegel and I am a UX Designer and UX Researcher from Los Angeles, California. I graduated from UCLA (University of California, Los Angeles) with a Cognitive Science B.S. and a minor in Statistics.

I chose to pursue a career in User Experience for two reasons. First, it allows me to work in any industry. I am a lifelong learner and as the world becomes more interconnected with technological advancements, I seek new opportunites in a variety of fields such as healthcare, journalism, and entertainment.

Secondly, I love how UX needs both creativity and scientific methods. My work process relies heavily on both deep ideation and research from start to finish. No matter what industry I am designing in, my mindset is to design tools and tools are only tools if they are functional. Whether it is A/B testing, user interviews, or usability testing, I make sure at the end of the day, everything I design is optimized for the target users.

I look to contribute my UX services for companies or agencies that are data-driven and who have a great team enviornment. There is nothing I enjoy more than collaborating with people to create innovative products and solutions.

Please reach out if you would like to collaborate on a project, have any questions about my work, or just to say hello!
